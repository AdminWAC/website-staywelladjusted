      Consent to Chiropractic Care

      Consent to Chiropractic Care, Supportive Therapies, Massage, and Diagnostic Services

      Valid for all locations: CO & TX

      Introduction

      This document provides consent for chiropractic and related healthcare services offered by Well Adjusted Chiropractic and its team of licensed providers. Our goal is to deliver high-quality care in a safe, respectful, and informed manner.

      Care Providers

      Services may be rendered by a licensed Doctor of Chiropractic, licensed massage therapist, or other appropriately licensed healthcare professional acting within the scope of their credentials and under the policies of Well Adjusted Chiropractic.

      Scope of Services

      I understand that my care may include one or more of the following:

      • Chiropractic adjustments and joint manipulations
      • Massage therapy
      • Electromagnetic or percussive modalities (e.g., PEMF therapy, vibration plate)
      • Intersegmental traction, spinal decompression
      • Prenatal, postnatal, and pediatric chiropractic care
      • Nutritional consultations and supplementation recommendations
      • Wellness coaching
      • Diagnostic imaging (e.g., X-rays, ultrasound)
      • Other therapeutic services offered now or in the future

      Informed Consent

      I understand that chiropractic and supportive therapies have been shown to be safe and effective for many conditions. Potential risks may include: temporary soreness, stiffness, or fatigue (common); minor aggravation of existing symptoms (occasional); sprain, strain, or soft tissue injury (rare); neurological injury or stroke (extremely rare).

      Patient Rights and Expectations

      I may ask questions and express concerns at any time. I have the right to decline any part of my care. My provider will explain any alternatives and implications if I decline a part of my care.

      Consent Duration & Revocation

      This consent is valid for the entire duration of my care at any Well Adjusted Chiropractic location unless I revoke it in writing.

      Limitation of Liability

      I understand that individual results may vary and that no guarantee of specific outcomes has been made.

      Jurisdiction

      I agree that any disputes arising from services rendered shall be governed by the laws of the state in which the care was provided.
