How chiropractic helps

Your nervous system runs through your spine.

Every signal your brain sends to your body, and every signal your body sends back, travels through nerves that exit between the bones of your spine. When those bones are misaligned, the nervous system can be affected. Here is the simple chain we look at.

01

Stress accumulates in your spine

Long hours at a desk, lifting, repetitive motion, sleep posture, falls, even daily stress. Your spine absorbs all of it.

02

Vertebrae can shift out of alignment

When a vertebra moves out of its proper position, it changes how the entire structure of your spine functions and moves.

03

That can irritate nearby nerves

Nerves exit between every pair of vertebrae. Misalignment can create the kind of nervous system interference your body feels downstream.

04

Restoring alignment removes interference

Chiropractic adjustments are designed to restore proper position and motion, supporting the body's natural ability to heal and regulate itself.
